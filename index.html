Bio

  • DJ and producer under the moniker halon (Deep House / Tech House / House)
  • Co-member of the "The Clockmakers" as DJ Bespoke ( Techno / DnB / Dubstep )
  • Co-member of "Cell Culture" (Atmospheric DnB)

Based in San Diego, CA. Past residences in San Francisco, CA and Dallas, TX.

Starting out under the moniker DJ3 circa 1996, James aka halon was a resident at Record Resource and played venues/events in Denton and Dallas TX. Moving to San Francisco in 1999, James started running weekly events with For The Music and then helped found the music side of False Profit. James ran the sound system for False Profit at 43 Norfolk and helped put on many amazing nights at the warehouse. He has also joined forces with many different crews and venues, including Dub Beautiful, Ambient Airlines, Space Cowboys, B-Family, Eklektic, Future Breaks, Tenderloft, Tags Trance Trip, GrooveTech Radio, Anon Salon, Trust, Wish, Mighty SF, DNA Lounge, 1015 Folsom, and many more.

In 2004, James help co-found Cedub Studios with owner Chris Walcott. During this time James moved away from djing to focus on production and engineering for local artists. James helped create and launch Fake Science LLC. first, as a podcast, then expanded the company to online digital music distribution and A&R. He produced tracks under the Cell Culture act with his co-founder Chris Walcott. He also began producing tracks as The Clockmakers with DJ Yap, under the moniker DJ Bespoke.

In 2009, James moved from the Bay and headed to his now home, San Diego. After a much too long hiatus, James is back on the 1s and 2s, bringing his love for djing and playing on freaking massive sound systems.
